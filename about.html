Click here for Slender: Eight Pages Official (The original slender game)

Slender is a short, experimental horror game in which you must travel into a dark woods at nigh, with a simple goal: find 8 pages that are scattered accross the woods, But as you are looking for them, you are being stalked by an entity that grows more persistant and relentless as each page is collected, an entity that you cannot even look at for long, let alone fight. Your only hope is to collect all 8 pages before it catches you...
