NEWS (17/02/2024)

So, the first playable build is out in the wild. Very few friends of mine are testing, finding bugs, etc. The programming side is now completed, and it's just polishing and filling in the few models I'm still missing. I am hoping to release by the end of next week, but I cannot guarantee. Some might notice the VR button; this is going to be a feature but not on launch. Why? Because I want it to be a well-polished edition with a player belt to put pages and the torch/other extras, and to include standalone versions for the Quest 2 and 3. Why not 1? Mainly because of performance. It could be done, but because I don't own a Quest 1, it makes it too hard to guarantee proper performance. So, I do apologize in advance for any Quest 1 players.
